Corporate Constitution

This document outlines the fundamental principles and operational rules by which The Juzz operates, ensuring its commitment to integrity, independence, and public service.

Core Operating Principles

  • • Will never feature advertisements or accept corporate sponsorships.
  • • Will never operate as a paid service; all content will remain free and accessible.
  • • Will always remain a non-profit entity.
  • • The CEO/Founder and the Board will never derive personal profit from the operation of this site.
  • • Will never relinquish decision control (e.g., will never have majority shareholders) and will always remain a private entity.
  • • Will only store data strictly required for site operation and will never sell or freely provide any user data to third parties.
  • • Will operate as an open-source project, promoting transparency and collaborative development.
  • • Will maintain full financial transparency, publicly disclosing how all funds are utilized.
  • • Will uphold content neutrality, fostering open dialogue and diverse perspectives, provided users operate with goodwill and adhere to community guidelines.

Edge Case Resolution & Transparency

In situations involving unforeseen circumstances or complex interpretations of these principles, the Board of Directors will convene to make decisions. All such decisions, and the rationale behind them, will be transparently documented and made publicly available to maintain accountability and trust.
